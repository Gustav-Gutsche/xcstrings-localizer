Frequently asked questions

Is the app store screenshot generator free?

Yes. StoreLocalizer's screenshot generator is free and open-source. It runs entirely in your browser, so there is no account, subscription, or upload to a server required to create and export your screenshots.

Does it add a watermark to my screenshots?

No. Exported screenshots contain only your own artwork, device frames, and captions. There is no watermark, logo, or branding added to the images.

Which device frames are available?

You can wrap your raw screenshots in iPhone and iPad device frames for the App Store, plus Android phone frames for Google Play. The 3D device rendering is done client-side with Three.js so frames look sharp at full export resolution.

Can the captions be translated into other languages?

Yes. The screenshot generator pairs with StoreLocalizer's App Store and Google Play localization tools, so the headlines and captions you add can be translated into 40+ languages with AI and reused across every localized screenshot set.

Which export sizes does it produce?

It exports the image dimensions required by App Store Connect and the Google Play Console, including the standard iPhone, iPad, and Android phone screenshot sizes, so you can upload the files directly without resizing.

Do my screenshots get uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your raw screenshots, captions, and exported files never leave your device, which keeps unreleased designs private.
